🧠 What Is Psychic Phenomena?


Psychic phenomena refer to abilities or experiences that defy conventional science, often linked to the mind’s hidden potential. These include:

  • Telepathy – reading another person's thoughts.
  • Clairvoyance – seeing events or objects beyond normal perception.
  • Precognition – knowing the future before it happens.
  • Psychokinesis – moving objects with the mind.
  • Remote Viewing – visualizing distant or hidden targets with uncanny precision.

They are the superpowers we’ve all dreamt of. But... could any of it be real?



🧬 Science vs. the Sixth Sense


The scientific community often dismisses psychic claims as pseudoscience due to a lack of reproducible evidence. However…

  • In the 1970s, the CIA and U.S. military launched Project Stargate, using remote viewers during the Cold War.
  • Some results? Classified. Others? Shockingly accurate.
  • The SRI experiments yielded drawings and insights into unseen locations—unexplained by coincidence.

Are governments just pretending? Or do they know something we don’t?
